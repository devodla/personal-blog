Bio

Full Stack Engineer

I am a Canadian software developer living in sunny and congested Los Angeles.

I currently work at a Software and Security company developing the front end of a business management web application using Angular.

I create highly interactive and reactive user interfaces to display and manipulate large amounts of data by communicating with a REST API.

On the back end, my favorite language is Ruby and I love building websites and web applications with Rails.

I have a Bachelor of Engineering degree from the University of Victoria (Victoria, BC).
